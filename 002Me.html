Jonathan is an emerging American artist who goes by the alias Auroreon.

He started with only strong and imaginative writing skills, only to thrist for greater power over realizing the full creative space. Specializing in high concept designs and trail blazing methodologies, Jonathan seeks to be a force to be reckoned with for world building and character designs across mixed medias such as videogames, film and research.

Jonathan currently exhibits his work at Collin College's exhibits and is starting to promote a presence on social media. Mastering new techniques, networking within many spaces and building toward being a key member of a studio, Jonathan is in high pursuit of owning his own legacy as a driver for a multitide of design implements and overwhelming collaborative spaces such as hackathons, galas and other venues.
